LeVar Burton, host of the educational children's show Reading Rainbow, is encouraging viewers to read banned books.

In addition to acting in landmark television shows like Roots and Star Trek: The Next Generation, Burton hosted and executive produced Reading Rainbow for PBS. In it, Burton and a celebrity guest would read from a selected children's book, discuss the themes of the book through field segments and reviews from adolescent readers. Reading Rainbow aired 155 episodes from 1983 to 2006, with reruns airing through 2009.

In 2014, Burton launched a Kickstarter for Skybrary by Reading Rainbow, an app that offered eBooks and videos "designed to engage young readers and foster a love of reading." The Kickstarter ended with more than 105,000 backers pledging over $6 million to the app, becoming the most backed Kickstarter in the website's history. However, due to a lawsuit by PBS affiliate WNED, Skybrary is no longer a license of the Reading Rainbow brand.
